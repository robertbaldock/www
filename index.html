About

Permanent Link

I am a first year PhD student in the Theory of Condensed Matter Group, in the Department of Physics at the University of Cambridge, where I am supervised by Professor MC Payne.

My research so far has been loosely related to diffusion processes on weighted, directed networks. I am currently addressing the question of whether the energy landscapes of proteins in the force-field picture, can be said to be small worlds or not when represented as a kinetic transition network in the manner developed by Professor David Wales and his group.
